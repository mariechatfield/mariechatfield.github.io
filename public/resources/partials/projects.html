Dr. Fractionstein's Laboratory

Collaborators: Katherine Ford
October 2013

Awarded 2nd Place in the 2013 CrossRealms Raspberry Pi Bake-Off.

Dr. Fractionstein's Laboratory is a fraction game for fourth and fifth graders.Using Dr. Fractionstein's Laboratory, students should demonstrate the following skills:

  • Add fractions of like denominators
  • Add fractions of unlike denominators
  • Find the least common denominator of a pair of fractions
  • Convert between different scales of fractions

Dr. Fractionstein’s Laboratory encourages students to improve their fraction and critical thinking skills in a novel way, with colorful graphics and silly potion names to retain attention and stimulate students’ imagination.

Google Maps GUI Harness

Developed for CodeSkulptor, used in Fall 2013 Rice University COMP 140 course.
